What's New

  • 2022 Grad

    Applegarth Elementary School teaches grades 4 and 5. The grade 5 students will graduate from 5th grade and go in to middle school at the end of their 5th grade year. Applegarth hosted a graduation for the 2022 5th grade students. The teachers would give opening and ending remarks, giving advice and words of good like to the students that are graduaiting. The students would be gifted a certificate and they would get to say a sentence about their favorite memory in their 5th grade year, or a thank you to who ever they would like to thank. At the end they would get a chance to talk to their parents and take photos with their friends, family, and teachers.The 2022 5th grade grads will soon go in to a different school. They would be entering middle school. The chapter of elementary closed for all of the 2022 5th grade grads.

  • Accelerated Math

    Applegarth Elementary School has a program in math which is made for students who are good at math. To get into this program, you have to qualify to a test. If you qualify to take the test, there is a set date on when you are going to take this test. To get in you have to get a certain score of 92 or above on the test. Even if you get a 92 or above there are a few more standards. Only if you get the score of 92 or above on the test and qualify for one of the two criteria (or both) you will qualify for the program. This program has a different math teacher. The students that are in this program will learn advanced math. For example, if you are in 5th grade and you are in Accelerated Math you will leave your classroom for one period and go to a different classroom. In that classroom, you learn advanced math with other students that are also in Accelerated Math. 5th grade learns 6th grade math. This program continues on until middle school. The last chance to get into Acclerated Math is in middle school in 7th grade.

  • Specials

    Applegarth Elementary School has a bunch of different classes. Other than the normal classes. Applegarth has gym, music, library, & art classes. Everyone one takes these classes once in a week. Everyone has 2 days of gym. During gym, you play fun games and activities. During music, you play some instruments, and sing, and learn new songs. In library, you learn new facts and check out 2 books you want to borrow fro the school. During art, you make art and learn about new artists. Other than these specials you can choose to do band or chorus. These are optional activities. For both chorus and band you have to go to school and hour early to practice before everyone else starts coming in for the normal school day. During chorus you learn new songs and practice them for the school concert. Before you start band, you have to choose an instrument to play. You learn new pieces of music for the concert. Doing band takes away from your normal school day time because you have pullout lessons to go to. These are the special classes we have in Applegarth Elementary School.
